The King of the Fourth

An Interactive Feature on Isaiah Thomas

Isaiah Thomas has been a revelation this season. Despite standing at just 5’9’’, he’s averaging 29 points and 6 assists a game, his team is the number one seed in the Eastern conference, and he just might crack the top five on the league’s MVP ballot.

The Numbers Don't Lie

Thomas is having one of the most efficient high-volume scoring seasons ever. Out of players who've taken at least 19 shots per game in a season, Thomas ranks 6th in True Shooting Percentage all-time and 2nd all-time among guards – only trailing Stephen Curry’s 2015-16 season.
